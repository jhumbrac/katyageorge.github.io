Our Story

As girls growing up we all had this wondrous moment of discovering the treasures of our mother's vanity, closet and jewelry bureau. Stilettos seven sizes too big, smeared lipstick and a string of pearls were met with laughter. However looking back at this first attempt at femininity, we still remember how happy it made us feel! For us growing up this nostalgia for unabashed self-expression was transformed into fascination with costume jewelry and especially the vintage masterpieces...

Vintage Jewelry

Posh Rx carries a variety of fashion jewelry brands from 1950-80's including Larry Vrba, Fendi, Valentino and Kenneth Jay Lane. Each piece arrives to us from auctions, antique flea markets, independent vendors and estate sales. All jewelry we carry is hand picked and carefully examined for defects before getting a stamp of approval for shipping to you. However it must be recognized that our vintage pieces date back to 1950-1980's and, thus, a certain extent of natural wear is to be expected...
